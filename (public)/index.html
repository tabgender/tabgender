about tabgender

Since January 1st, 2023, more than 500 anti-trans bills have been introduced across our country.
In 2022, 174 anti-trans bills were introduced.
Transgender people are in danger.
tabgender aims to help raise awareness and funds to serve the transgender community.
From ad revenue on our Google homepage, we donate 100% of our profits to organizations that support transgender rights.
Every time you interact with our page, you raise money to support trans people. We hope that, by advocating for trans people, we can contribute to a movement to defeat transphobia.
